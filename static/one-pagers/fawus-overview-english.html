Program Overview

FAWUS is a comprehensive year-long international fellowship starting April 1st, 2026, designed for experienced POCUS clinicians who want to become ultrasound educators, researchers, and global leaders. This unique program combines clinical training, teaching experience, research opportunities, and international networking.

Apr 1, 2026 Start Date

Program Highlights

  • Virtual Core Curriculum: Self-paced WINFOCUS Academy modules with comprehensive ultrasound content
  • Clinical Training: Complete 500+ verified scans with structured mentor feedback
  • Teaching Experience: Lead webinars, lectures, workshops, and create educational videos
  • Research Project: Complete one academic project (case report, QI, or publication)
  • Global Network: Connect with WINFOCUS faculty across 176 countries
  • Official Certificate: Earn FAWUS title and priority for future faculty roles

Who Should Apply?

  • Physicians in EM, IM, ICU, anesthesia, pediatrics, or family medicine
  • Clinicians with POCUS experience and passion for education
  • Junior faculty with strong ultrasound background
  • Healthcare professionals interested in academic growth and global impact

Eligibility Requirements

  • Medical degree from accredited institution
  • Valid medical license
  • Basic POCUS training and regular use in practice
  • English proficiency (spoken and written)
  • Local mentor support

Program Structure

Core Activities:

  • Online WINFOCUS Academy coursework
  • 500+ scan log with mentor verification
  • Monthly mentorship sessions
  • Minimum 2 teaching sessions
  • One regional course participation
  • Academic project completion
  • Final exam + graduation ceremony

Format:

  • Primarily virtual and flexible
  • Based at your current institution
  • Local and global mentor support
  • Weekly access to didactics at Global Partner Institutes
  • Self-paced with structured milestones

What You'll Receive

  • Complete WINFOCUS Academy access
  • Personalized mentorship from global experts
  • Official FAWUS Certificate and title
  • Presentation opportunities at international conferences
  • Priority consideration for future WINFOCUS faculty positions
  • Lifetime membership in global ultrasound educator network
